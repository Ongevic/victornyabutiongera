Research

My research spans multiple areas of artificial intelligence and machine learning, with a focus on developing practical solutions for real-world problems.

Machine Learning Optimization

Developing novel optimization algorithms for deep neural networks to improve training efficiency and model performance. This includes work on adaptive learning rates, regularization techniques, and architecture search.

Deep Learning Optimization Neural Architecture Search

Computer Vision

Research in image recognition, object detection, and scene understanding using advanced deep learning techniques. Applications include medical imaging, autonomous vehicles, and environmental monitoring.

Image Recognition Object Detection Medical Imaging

Natural Language Processing

Working on language models, text generation, and understanding for improved human-computer interaction. Focus areas include sentiment analysis, machine translation, and conversational AI.

Language Models Text Generation Sentiment Analysis

AI Ethics & Explainability

Investigating the ethical implications of AI systems and developing methods to make machine learning models more interpretable and transparent for end users and stakeholders.

AI Ethics Explainable AI Model Interpretation
